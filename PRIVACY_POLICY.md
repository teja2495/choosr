# Privacy Policy for Choosr

**Last Updated:** 11/24/2025

## Introduction

Choosr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Choosr stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Choice Lists**: Lists you create, including:
  - List names
  - Items in each list
  - Emoji icons (if added)
  - Color preferences (if set)
- **App Preferences**: 
  - View type preference (grid or list)
  - "Avoid previous results" setting

### Data We Do NOT Collect

Choosr does **NOT** collect, transmit, or share:

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Crash reports
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data transmitted over the internet

## How We Use Your Information

All data stored by Choosr remains on your device and is used solely to:

- Display your choice lists and items
- Remember your preferences
- Enable the random selection/shuffle functionality
- Allow you to export and import your data

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- You have full control over your data through the export/import feature

## Data Export and Import

Choosr allows you to:

- **Export your data**: Create a backup file containing all your lists and preferences
- **Import your data**: Restore your lists and preferences from a backup file

Export and import operations are performed entirely on your device. You choose where to save or load backup files.

## Third-Party Services

Choosr uses the following third-party libraries:

- **Gson** (Google): Used for local JSON serialization/deserialization. This library does not transmit any data and operates entirely on your device.

No other third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks are integrated into the App.

## Permissions

Choosr does not request any special permissions from your device. The App only uses standard Android functionality to:

- Store data locally on your device
- Access files you explicitly choose to import/export (via Android's file picker)

## Children's Privacy

Choosr does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data, this is not applicable.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App or updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You can delete all data by uninstalling the App
- You can export your data at any time
- You can delete individual lists or items within the App
- You have complete control over your data

## Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by Choosr:

1. Uninstall the App from your device, or
2. Clear the App's data through your device's Settings > Apps > Choosr > Storage > Clear Data
